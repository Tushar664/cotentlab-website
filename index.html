Straight Answers

Questions every creator asks us.

What results can I realistically expect?

Across coaching and EdTech niches our campaigns typically land between 4x and 12x ROAS once the system stabilises — usually within the first 60–90 days. We'll never promise a specific number before auditing your offer; anyone who does is guessing with your money.

How much ad budget do I need to start?

Enough to generate statistically meaningful data — for most creators that's a starting range we'll define together on the diagnosis call based on your ticket size and funnel math. Performance marketing without meaningful spend simply doesn't perform.

I've never run ads before. Is that a problem?

No — some of our best results came from accounts we built from zero. What matters is your offer and audience. The ads, funnel, automation and webinar training are exactly what we bring.

What if my webinar doesn't convert?

That's the module most agencies ignore and we obsess over. We train you on structure, delivery and pitch until it converts consistently — in one recent case, two weeks of focused iteration took a webinar from unsustainable to reliably profitable.

Do you lock me into a contract?

No lock-ins. We keep clients by making them money, not by making them sign. Our retention rate is the proof.

Can you guarantee results?

No honest agency can legally guarantee ROAS. What we guarantee: the complete CoTent System™, daily optimisation, full transparency on every rupee — and a track record where most clients multiply their revenue within months.
